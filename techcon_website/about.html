History

TechCon was founded in 2010 with the aim of bringing together the brightest minds in technology to share their knowledge and innovations. Over the years, it has grown from a small local event to a major international conference attended by thousands of tech enthusiasts and professionals.

Mission

The mission of TechCon is to foster innovation and collaboration within the tech community. We strive to provide a platform where industry leaders, developers, and enthusiasts can come together to exchange ideas, explore new technologies, and drive the industry forward.

Elon Musk, CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, shared his visionary insights on space exploration and sustainable energy at TechCon 2018.

Sundar Pichai
